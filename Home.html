Our Recommendations

Classroom of the Elite

The Japanese government has established the Tokyo Metropolitan Advanced Nurturing School, dedicated to instruct and foster the generation of people that will support the country in the future. The students are given a high degree of freedom in order to closely mimic real life.

Wind Breaker

Sakura is a loner and a high school delinquent who wants to be a fighter at the top of the world. He makes his way to Furin High School, a high school infamously known for its delinquents. now known as the "Chime of the Wind Breaker" - Bofurin.

Shangri-la Frontier

Shangri-La Frontier or SLF is a VRMMORPG open world game whose main story involves exploring a vast world. Players assume assorted classes, completing story scenarios. A year after the launch of the game, its popularity had reached its new max of 30 million accounts.

Spy x Family

The story follows a spy who has to "build a family" to execute a mission, not realizing that his adopted daughter is a telepath, and the woman he agrees to marry is a skilled assassin.

Kaguya-sama Love is War

The proudly privileged top two students of an elite school each makes it their mission to be the first to extract a confession of love from the other. With later chapters developing the other members of the Student Council.
